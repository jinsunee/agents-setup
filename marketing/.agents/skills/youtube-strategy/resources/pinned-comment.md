---

## Pinned Comment vs Description

| Aspect | Description | Pinned Comment |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| **Visibility** | Hidden behind "Show More" | Always visible at top |
| **Purpose** | SEO + context + resources | Engagement + conversation |
| **Tone** | Professional, structured | Conversational, personal |
| **Length** | Can be long | Under 100 words ideal |
| **Audience** | Viewers deciding to watch | Viewers who already watched |
| **Links** | Multiple organized links | 1-4 focused links max |

**Description = Reference Guide** (complete resources, timestamps, SEO)
**Pinned Comment = Conversation Starter** (one action, immediate value, discussion)

---

## The Pinned Comment Formula

A great pinned comment has **2-3 of these elements**:

1. **Clear purpose** - Why are you pinning this?
2. **Immediate value** - Something viewers want/need now
3. **Call to action** - What should they do?
4. **Personality** - Your authentic voice
5. **Scannability** - Readable in 3 seconds

## Timing Strategy

**Post immediately if your comment contains:**
- Required resources (repo links, dependencies)
- Known issues or critical fixes
- Scope clarification to prevent confusion

**Post later when you need to:**
- Respond to patterns in viewer confusion
- Address emerging debate
- Announce follow-up content

**Replace your pinned comment when:**
- Discussion shifts to a new focal point
- You discover a better solution
- Breaking news makes your approach outdated
